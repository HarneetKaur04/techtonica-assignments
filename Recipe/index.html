Blueberries Ice Cream

Home Recipes

One can never say no to ice cream. I never knew it's so easy to make ice cream at home using very few ingredients. It gives me the flexibility to customize it to my taste buds an enjoy it guilt free. So, enjoy it while it melts.

Photo Credit: Glyco Leap

List of Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe bananas, peeled and cut into 1-inch rounds
  • 2 cups frozen blueberries
  • 1/4 cup coconut milk or natural plain yogurt
  • Mint to garnish

Steps to Follow:

  1. Peel bananas, cut into bite-sized pieces, and freeze with blueberries for at least 5 hours or overnight.
  2. Stir coconut milk well, pour into ice cube tray, and freeze for at least 5 hours or overnight.
  3. Puree frozen bananas, blueberries, and coconut ice cubes in a high-performance food processor until creamy.
  4. Garnish with fresh mint leaves to taste and serve immediately.
